Egg Burgi

Description

Egg Burgi is an indian take on a traditional scrambled egg that provides more spice and flavour to the recipe. Utilizing the use of heavy whipping cream and spices, the egg ends up tasting like a proper dish rather than a boring time saving slop of Egg

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 3 to 4 chillis
  • teaspoon of chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 7 strands of cilantro
  • 1/3 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1-2 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 onion

Procedure

  1. Put pan on the stove to medium heat and add the butter to the pan
  2. Move the butter around and make sure pan is covered with the butter
  3. Finely chop the onions, garlic, cilantro, and chillis
  4. Add the chopped ingredients to the pan
  5. Crack the eggs and put them into a bowl to be whisked
  6. Whisk eggs till the mixture is completely yellow and add to the pan
  7. Add the chilli powder and salt
  8. Once egg starts to clump up,turn the heat to low and add the whipping cream
  9. Moderate the heat and roll the egg around now, aim to avoid overcooking one side
  10. Keep overlooking egg till it is made the way you want and Boom
  11. Finished
